About Us

Our Description

R2(CM) IT Training Agency Inc. is an industry-oriented institute that offers innovative training programs, in collaboration with the industry, designed to bridge the gap between current academic offerings and the job market. R2(CM) is committed to the vision of building sustainable careers for the future IT leaders of our country.

We also have a business model that is designed to help companies save on recruitment, hiring and training costs and at the same time hire a work-ready and well-equipped resource. We do this by changing the paradigm in preparing resources joining a company. Resources undergo essential IT skills combined with professionalism training before they join a company, increasing productivity upon hiring and accelerating a company's go-to-market strategy.


Objectives

R2(CM) aims to assist trainees to jumpstart their IT career, increase prospects for a better compensation and find the right job in an organization. This allows trainees to experience firsthand true office situations helping them perform better and efficient once they join the real-life corporate world.


Vision

R2(CM) was established with a vision to enhance employment among fresh graduates by providing complete technical and soft skills training to help them sustain high performance and attain success in their careers and eventually adapt to the growing IT industry demand for the right talent at the entry-level.


Mission

The mission of our training is to promote and support trainee development and organizational effectiveness by providing high-quality educational training programs. Trainings are designed to meet individual, group or departmental and institutional needs and objectives. We strive to enhance individual learning and development as the means for creating a better workplace environment.
